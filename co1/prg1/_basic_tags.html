MCA

Master of computer application is a type of postgraduate academic master's degree The programme is a blend of both theorectical and practical knowledge.

Students pursuing an MCAprogramme would have the option to choose from from the following specialisation:

  • Application software
  • Hardware Technology
  • Internet
  • Management Information Systems
  • Networking

ELIGIBILITY criteria for MCA

According to AICTE,to pursue an MCA course candicates must have pursued BCA/Bsc/Bcom/BA degree with Mathematics as one of subject at 10+2 level or at graduation
